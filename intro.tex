\section{Introduction}\label{sec:intro}

Derivatives of regular expressions (REs) as introduced by Brzozowski~\cite{Brzozowski64,OwensEtAl09}
provide a very compact algorithm for generating deterministic finite automata (DFA).

Inspired by Brzozowski's work, Antimirov~\cite{Antimirov96} defined partial derivatives of REs to define a simple algorithm to generate non-deterministic finite automata (NFA) with a limited number of states.
